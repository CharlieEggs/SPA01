COMP1004 Charlie Eggs A.C.D.F.

A.C.D.F means Agent of the Control Defence Force. They are charged with the protection of the earth through more... clandestine operations. During this game you will be evaluated for your suitability to become an A.C.D.F. where upon ending the game you will be accpeted into the organisation or you will be removed.

The game is simply a text adventure where you will progress by selecting the options you prefer, from choosing your equipment to choosing what kind of agent you want to be. Choose carefully.
