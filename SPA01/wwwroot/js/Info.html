What is the C.D.F.?

The Control Defence Force, or C.D.F., is the most secret organisation in the world. It exists to protect the world from themselves, solving problems on every continent.

What is this scenario?

The C.D.F. is full of the most skilled people in the world at what they do. These scenarios are designed to test every decision making reflex you have. You will be tested on tactical thinking, reasoning, stealth and much more.

How will the scenario work?

The scenario is designed like a retro text-adventure. You will proceed through the adventure choosing every action as you go, if by the end you are deemed a good fit you will be accepted into the ranks of the C.D.F.
